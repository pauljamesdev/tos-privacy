# Privacy Policy  

_Last updated: March 27, 2025_  

Your privacy is important to us. This policy outlines how we handle data.  

## 1. Information We Collect  
We do not collect or store personal information. Our service only processes publicly available data from TikTok and Instagram.  

## 2. Data Usage  
We use TikTok and Instagram APIs to fetch trending content. We do not modify or store this data beyond temporary processing.  

## 3. Third-Party Services  
Our service depends on third-party APIs. Please review TikTok’s and Instagram’s privacy policies for details on their data handling.  

## 4. Changes to This Policy  
We may update this Privacy Policy. Continued use of our service means you accept the updated policy.  

## 5. Contact  
For questions, reach out to us via this GitHub repository.
